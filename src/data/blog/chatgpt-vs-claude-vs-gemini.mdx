## The Honest Comparison

Every AI company claims theirs is the best. None of them are lying, exactly — they're just measuring different things. Here's what actually matters when you're trying to pick one (or two) to use daily.

## The Quick Overview

| | ChatGPT | Claude | Gemini |
|---|---------|--------|--------|
| **Made by** | OpenAI | Anthropic | Google |
| **Flagship model** | GPT-5.4 | Opus 4.6 | 2.0 Ultra |
| **Free tier** | GPT-5.4 mini | Sonnet | Standard |
| **Paid price** | $20/month | $20/month | $20/month |
| **Context window** | 1M tokens | 200K tokens | 2M tokens |
| **Image generation** | Yes (DALL-E) | No | Yes |
| **Voice mode** | Yes | No | Yes |
| **App integrations** | Spotify, Uber, etc | Limited | Google Workspace |
| **Best at** | All-round, images, apps | Reasoning, coding, writing | Google integration, research |

## Where Each One Wins

### ChatGPT Wins At:

**Breadth of features.** ChatGPT has the widest feature set: image generation, voice conversations, [app integrations](/news/2026-03-15-chatgpt-app-integrations-spotify-uber-doordash/) (Spotify, Uber, DoorDash), custom GPTs, and [computer use](/news/2026-03-14-openai-gpt-5-4-computer-use/). If you want one tool that does everything adequately, ChatGPT is hard to beat.

**Image generation.** DALL-E 3 integration means you can generate and edit images directly in the conversation. Neither Claude nor Gemini (base) match this for creative image work.

**Ecosystem size.** More third-party tools, plugins, and integrations than any other AI platform. If you need your AI to connect to external services, ChatGPT has the most options.

### Claude Wins At:

**Reasoning quality.** For complex analysis, multi-step problems, and tasks requiring careful thinking, Claude consistently produces more thoughtful, nuanced responses. It follows complex instructions more precisely than the competition.

**Coding.** Claude is the strongest coding assistant, especially through [Claude Code](/tutorials/how-to-use-claude-code/) (the terminal agent). It writes cleaner code, catches more edge cases, and handles multi-file changes better.

**Long document analysis.** While its context window (200K tokens) is smaller than GPT-5.4's (1M) or Gemini's (2M), Claude uses that context more effectively. It's better at finding relevant information in long documents.

**Writing quality.** Claude produces the most natural-sounding text with the least "AI voice." For content that needs to sound human, Claude wins. See our guide on [making AI writing sound natural](/blog/how-to-make-ai-writing-sound-natural/).

### Gemini Wins At:

**Google integration.** If you use Gmail, Google Docs, Sheets, Drive, and Calendar, Gemini integrates directly. Ask it to find a specific email, summarise a Drive document, or check your calendar — [no tab switching needed](/news/2026-03-12-google-gemini-workspace-march-update/).

**Free tier generosity.** Gemini's free tier is the most generous of the three. More messages, access to image generation, and integration with Google services at no cost.

**Context window.** Gemini 2.0 Ultra's [2-million-token context window](/news/google-gemini-2-ultra-launch/) is the largest available. For processing extremely long documents, entire codebases, or hours of video, Gemini handles the biggest inputs.

**Multimodal understanding.** Native support for text, images, video, and audio in a single conversation. You can upload a video and ask questions about it.

## Use Case Recommendations

| If you primarily... | Use | Why |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Need an all-purpose assistant | ChatGPT | Widest features, good at everything |
| Write code | Claude | Best code quality, Claude Code agent |
| Write content | Claude | Most natural writing, best instruction-following |
| Use Google Workspace | Gemini | Native integration with Gmail, Docs, Drive |
| Do research | Perplexity | Built for search with sources |
| Generate images | ChatGPT | DALL-E 3 integration |
| Analyse long documents | Gemini or Claude | Largest context (Gemini) or best accuracy (Claude) |
| Are on a budget | Gemini | Most generous free tier |

## The Real Answer

Most power users don't pick just one. The combination that works for most people:

1. **Claude** for serious work (coding, analysis, writing)
2. **ChatGPT** for quick tasks and image generation
3. **Perplexity** for research

All three have free tiers. Try each for a week, see which fits your workflow, and go from there. If you're only going to pay for one, pick the one that matches what you do most.
