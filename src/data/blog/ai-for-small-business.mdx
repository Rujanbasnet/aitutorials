## The Solopreneur Advantage

Here's the thing nobody talks about: AI benefits small businesses more than big ones.

Large companies have departments for everything — marketing, support, design, development. Small businesses have one person wearing all those hats. AI lets that one person perform like a small team.

## Where to Start: The High-Impact Tasks

### 1. Content Creation

Stop staring at blank pages. Use AI to:
- Draft blog posts from an outline (then edit in your voice)
- Write social media posts from a single piece of content
- Create email newsletters
- Generate product descriptions

The key is using AI for the first draft, then adding your personality. We cover this in depth in our guide on [making AI writing sound natural](/blog/how-to-make-ai-writing-sound-natural/).

### 2. Customer Support

AI chatbots can handle 60-80% of customer questions instantly:
- FAQ responses
- Order status inquiries
- Business hours and location
- Basic troubleshooting

Tools like Intercom, Drift, and Tidio offer AI chatbots starting at $0-30/month.

### 3. Email Marketing

AI writes email sequences that would take hours:

```
Write a 3-email welcome sequence for new subscribers to 
[my business type]. Tone: friendly and professional. 
Include a soft sell for [product/service] in email 3.
```

Combine with tools like [Brevo](https://www.brevo.com/) (free tier: 300 emails/day) for a complete email marketing system at zero cost.

### 4. Bookkeeping and Invoicing

AI-powered tools like QuickBooks AI and Xero can:
- Categorise expenses automatically
- Generate invoices from conversation
- Predict cash flow
- Flag unusual transactions

### 5. Website and Tools

[Vibe coding](/non-coder/what-is-vibe-coding/) lets you build your own website, booking system, or internal tools without hiring a developer. Tools like [Claude Code](/tutorials/how-to-use-claude-code/) can build and maintain a professional website for the cost of an API subscription.

## The 30-Minute Setup

1. **Sign up for Claude or ChatGPT** ($20/month or free tier)
2. **Identify your biggest time sink** — the task you dread most
3. **Write a prompt** — describe exactly what you need ([prompting cheatsheet](/resources/ai-prompting-cheatsheet/))
4. **Test the output** — edit, refine, and judge the quality
5. **Automate or repeat** — make it part of your weekly workflow

## Common Mistakes Small Businesses Make with AI

1. **Trying to automate everything at once** — pick one task, master it, then expand
2. **Publishing raw AI output** — always edit and add your voice
3. **Using AI for tasks that need human touch** — complaints, sensitive conversations, relationship building
4. **Not learning prompting basics** — 5 minutes learning [prompt engineering](/tutorials/prompt-engineering-fundamentals/) saves hours of bad output
5. **Ignoring free tiers** — most tools offer enough for a small business to get started at $0
