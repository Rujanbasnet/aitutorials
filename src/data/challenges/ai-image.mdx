## What you'll create

Stunning, professional-quality images — from photorealistic portraits to digital art to logo concepts. No design skills required. Just the right words.

## The formula

Great AI image prompts follow a pattern. Here's the formula:

**[Subject] + [Style] + [Lighting] + [Mood] + [Technical details]**

Most people just type "a cat" and wonder why the results look mediocre. The details are what separate amateur prompts from professional ones.

## Prompt 1: Photorealistic portrait

Paste this into an image generator:

```text
Professional headshot portrait of a confident young entrepreneur in a modern co-working space, soft natural window lighting, shallow depth of field, warm color tones, shot on Canon R5 85mm f/1.4, editorial magazine style
```

## Prompt 2: Digital art / illustration

```text
Mystical floating island with ancient Japanese temple, cherry blossoms falling through clouds, golden hour lighting, Studio Ghibli inspired art style, highly detailed digital painting, 4K resolution, atmospheric perspective
```

## Prompt 3: Product / marketing

```text
Minimal flat lay of a premium coffee brand, matte black packaging on white marble surface, single green leaf accent, soft overhead studio lighting, clean commercial photography, negative space for text overlay
```

## Prompt 4: Logo concept

```text
Minimalist logo design for a tech startup called "Nova", abstract geometric star shape, clean lines, midnight blue and electric purple gradient, vector style, white background, professional brand identity
```

## Where to generate

These prompts work across multiple AI image generators:

- **[ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com)** — Has DALL-E built in. Free users get limited generations, paid gets more.
- **[Gemini](https://gemini.google.com)** — Google's Imagen. Free to use, generates high-quality images.
- **[Ideogram](https://ideogram.ai)** — Free tier available. Excellent at text in images and logos.
- **[Leonardo AI](https://leonardo.ai)** — Free tier with 150 daily tokens. Great for variety of styles.

## What makes a great image prompt

| Element | Why it matters | Example |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| **Subject** | What's in the image | "a fox sitting on a mountain" |
| **Style** | The artistic approach | "watercolor painting", "3D render", "photograph" |
| **Lighting** | Sets the mood | "golden hour", "neon-lit", "soft studio lighting" |
| **Mood** | Emotional feel | "serene", "dramatic", "playful" |
| **Technical** | Quality cues | "4K", "highly detailed", "shallow depth of field" |

## What just happened

You gave the AI a detailed creative brief — the same kind of brief you'd give a professional photographer or designer. The more specific your description, the closer the output matches your vision.

Professional stock photos cost $10-50 each. Custom illustration starts at $200+. You just created unlimited visuals for free.
